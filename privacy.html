Privacy Policy

StakingForge collects only the information needed to respond to requests, provide services, process payments, and manage community access.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, company details, service interests, messages submitted through forms, payment status from payment providers, and Discord-related membership information.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to contact requests and consultation inquiries.
  • To provide market intelligence, infrastructure, advisory, and community access services.
  • To process subscriptions and manage paid access through third-party providers.
  • To improve website performance, security, and service quality.

Third-Party Providers

Payments, email delivery, analytics, scheduling, and Discord access may be handled by third-party services such as Stripe, Calendly, Discord, LaunchPass, Vercel, and email providers. Their own policies apply to data processed by them.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for business, support, compliance, and record-keeping purposes.
